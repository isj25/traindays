About Us

Your trusted companion for IRCTC booking planning

Our Mission

RailBookingDate was created with a simple mission: to make train travel planning in India less stressful. We know how critical it is to book tickets the moment reservations open, and our tool helps you stay ahead of the game.

What We Do

We provide a simple, accurate, and fast calculator for Indian Railways Advance Reservation Period (ARP) and Tatkal booking dates. Whether you are planning a trip 60 days in advance or trying to snag a last-minute Tatkal ticket, we tell you exactly when you need to be at your keyboard.

Why Choose Us?

Unlike complex apps, we focus on one thing and do it well. Our tool accounts for current IRCTC rules, including the different timings for AC and Sleeper classes, and the specific rules for Tatkal bookings.

The Maker

RailBookingDate is an independent project by Ishwar Joshi. As a frequent traveler, I built this tool to solve a personal pain point and decided to share it with the fellow passengers of Indian Railways.

Independent & Free

We are not affiliated with IRCTC or Indian Railways. We are an independent utility tool, and we intend to keep our services free and accessible for everyone.
