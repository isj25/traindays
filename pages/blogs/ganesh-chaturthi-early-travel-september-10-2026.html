Ganesh Chaturthi Early Travel Train Booking Guide (3–4 Days Before)

Ganesh Chaturthi Early Travel

Festival: Monday, September 14, 2026

Arrive 3–4 Days Before the Festival

When Should You Book?

Most families do not travel on the day of Ganesh Chaturthi itself. They leave 3 to 4 days earlier so they can help with preparations, bring prasad and decorations, and settle in before the first puja. If that sounds like your plan, your IRCTC booking dates are earlier than the festival-day guide—and missing that window is the most common booking mistake.

Ganesh Chaturthi falls on Monday, September 14, 2026. Below are the exact advance and Tatkal dates for the three most popular early-arrival travel days. Booking opens 60 days before your journey date, not 60 days before the festival.

Important: Advance reservation opens at 8:00 AM IST exactly 60 days before your train’s departure date. The earliest window for Ganesh season early travel is Sunday, July 12, 2026 (for a Thursday, September 10 departure)—four days before July 16, when booking opens for travel on the festival day itself.

Pro Tip: Konkan Railway, Mumbai–Pune, and Mumbai–Nagpur routes fill fastest for September 10–11 departures. Log in by 7:55 AM on Sunday, July 12, 2026 if you are targeting the earliest travel date—do not wait until July 16, which is only for trains departing on the festival day.

Why Most People Travel Early

  • Family preparations: Ganesh idol installation and decoration often start 2–3 days before Chaturthi.
  • Less rush than festival day: Monday Sep 14 sees a second wave of travelers; Thursday and Friday trains are busy but slightly more predictable if booked on time.
  • Return flexibility: Early arrivals can sometimes plan return travel after visarjan without competing with outbound festival-day crowds.

Traveling on Ganesh Chaturthi Day Instead?

If you plan to reach home on Monday, September 14, 2026 itself, advance booking opens on Thursday, July 16, 2026 at 8:00 AM. See our dedicated Ganesh Chaturthi (Sep 14) train booking guide.

Booking Checklist

  1. Decide your journey date first (Sep 10, 11, or 12)—booking dates depend on departure, not the festival date.
  2. Create an IRCTC account and add passengers to your Master List before the booking window.
  3. Keep UPI or net banking ready; premium routes can sell out within minutes.
  4. Use our Booking Date Calculator to verify dates for connecting journeys or return trips.
