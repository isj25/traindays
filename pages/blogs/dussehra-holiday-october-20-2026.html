Why Book Early for Dussehra Holiday?

Dussehra Holiday is one of the busiest travel times in India. Thousands of travelers will be booking tickets to visit their hometowns. Waiting for Tatkal is risky as seats are very limited.

  • Avoid Waitlists: Popular routes see waitlists reaching heavily within hours of opening.
  • Save Money: Dynamic pricing in Premium Tatkal can make tickets very expensive later.
  • Peace of Mind: Confirm your travel plans 2 months in advance.

Booking Checklist

  1. Create an IRCTC account if you don't have one.
  2. Add passengers to your 'Master List' on IRCTC to save time during booking.
  3. Keep your payment details (UPI/Netbanking) ready.
  4. Use our Booking Date Calculator to check dates for other journeys.
