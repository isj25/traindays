About Live Train Tracking

What is Live Station Status?

Live Train Running Status helps you spot your train's exact current location in real-time. It tells you which station the train has just crossed, whether it is running on time (Right Time - RT) or is delayed, and the estimated time of arrival (ETA) at upcoming stations.

How to Track Your Train?

  1. Find your 5-digit Train Number (e.g., 12951).
  2. Click on one of the tracking links above (e.g., NTES or RailYatri).
  3. Enter the Train Number in the search bar.
  4. Select the journey date (usually "Today" or "Yesterday").
  5. View the live dashboard to see current milestones and delay info.
