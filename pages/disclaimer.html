Official notice regarding RailBookingDate and its services

No Association with IRCTC

RailBookingDate is NOT associated, affiliated, or connected with IRCTC (Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation), Indian Railways, or any other government agency in any way.

This website is an independent tool designed solely to help users calculate train ticket booking dates (Advance Reservation Period and Tatkal timings) based on the publicly available rules of Indian Railways.

The official website for Indian Railways ticket booking is www.irctc.co.in. We do not provide ticket booking services, and we do not collect any personal information for booking purposes.

Accuracy of Information

While we strive to provide the most accurate and up-to-date information, the booking rules and Advance Reservation Periods (ARP) may change at the discretion of Indian Railways without prior notice.

Users are strongly advised to verify the calculated dates and booking timings with official sources such as the IRCTC website, the NTES (National Train Enquiry System) app, or at any Railway Reservation Counter before making travel plans.

RailBookingDate and its creators shall not be held responsible for any loss, inconvenience, or missed bookings resulting from inaccuracies in the calculations or information provided on this site. The use of this tool is at the user's own risk.
