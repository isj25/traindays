Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Indian Railways booking rules

What is the IRCTC advance reservation period?

IRCTC allows train tickets to be booked up to 60 days in advance, excluding the date of journey. The booking opens at 8:00 AM IST on the opening day.

How do I calculate the IRCTC booking opening date?

You can calculate the IRCTC booking opening date by subtracting 60 days from your journey date. To avoid manual errors, use our IRCTC booking date calculator to get the exact date instantly.

At what time does IRCTC ticket booking open?

IRCTC online ticket booking opens at 8:00 AM IST for all classes, including Sleeper, 3AC, 2AC, and 1AC.

Is the journey date included in the 60-day advance booking?

No. The journey date is not included while calculating the 60-day advance reservation period. Our calculator automatically follows IRCTC rules to give accurate results.

Can I book train tickets before 60 days?

No, IRCTC does not allow booking before 60 days (except special trains). Booking attempts before the opening date will fail.

No. Tatkal tickets have a different booking window (1 day before journey). This calculator is designed specifically for advance reservation tickets.
