Official Rule

What is the IRCTC advance reservation period?

IRCTC allows train tickets to be booked up to 60 days in advance, excluding the date of journey. The booking opens at 8:00 AM IST on the opening day.

New for 2026

What is the new Aadhaar verification rule for 2026?

From 2026, IRCTC has implemented stricter Aadhaar-linkage rules for advance reservations:

  • Online Booking Restriction: Only Aadhaar-authenticated IRCTC users will be allowed to book general reserved tickets online on the opening day of ARP (Advance Reservation Period).
  • Extended Window: Verified users can book tickets from 8:00 AM until midnight on the opening day.
  • Non-Verified Users: Users without Aadhaar authentication will not be able to book online on Day One and must wait until later, depending on seat availability.
  • Wider Window: This new rule significantly widens the booking window for Aadhaar-verified users compared to earlier limited time slots.

To avoid missing out on peak holiday bookings, we highly recommend linking your Aadhaar to your IRCTC profile immediately. You can follow the official steps here: Official Aadhaar Linking Guide (PDF)
