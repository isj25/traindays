IRCTC Helpline Services

One Number for One India - Complete support for Rail Users

Rail Madad Helpline

The integrated helpline number 139 is a single window for all enquiry and grievance redressal for rail users.

View Official IRCTC Contact Document

Key Benefits

  • Universal Access: Single window information through universal no. 139.
  • Multilingual Support: Interact with professionally trained agents in Hindi, English, and regional languages.
  • 24/7 Availability: Round-the-clock support for passengers.

Premium Services (SMS on 139)

Get information instantly by sending an SMS to 139 from your mobile. Use the following formats:

PNR Enquiry

PNR <10-digit PNR Number>

Example: PNR 1234567890

Train Arrival/Departure

AD <Train Number> <Station Code>

Example: AD 12002 NDLS

Accommodation Availability

AV <Train Number> <Date> <Source> <Dest> <Class> <Quota>

Example: AV 12002 250126 NDLS BPL CC G

Fare Enquiry

FARE <Train Number> <Date> <Source> <Dest> <Class> <Quota>

IRCTC Customer Support

For internet ticketing related issues (Cancellation, Payment, TDR, etc.):

  • Online eQuery Interface: equery.irctc.co.in (Submit and track queries)
  • E-Ticket / TDR Issues: etickets@irctc.co.in
  • Customer Care (Within India): 14646 (or) 0755-6610661
  • Customer Care (Outside India): +91-8044647999 / +91-8035734999

Multilingual Support

Support is available in: Hindi, English, Punjabi, Bengali, Assamese, Odia, Marathi, Gujarati, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada and Malayalam.
