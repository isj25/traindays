Tatkal Rules & Tips

  • Window: Tatkal opens 1 day before journey (from train origin logic as per IRCTC rules).
  • AC Classes: 1A, 2A, 3A, CC, EC, 3E open at 10:00 AM IST.
  • Sleeper/2S: SL and 2S open at 11:00 AM IST.
  • Per PNR limit: Max 4 passengers for Tatkal booking.
  • Faster checkout: Keep passenger master list and payment method ready before opening minute.
