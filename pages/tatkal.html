Tatkal Booking Dates

Know exactly which travel dates are open for Tatkal booking right now

Booking Date Travel Date Day

* Tatkal booking opens 1 day before the travel date (excluding the date of journey from source station).

Tatkal Booking Timings

  • AC Classes (1A, 2A, 3A, CC, EC, 3E): Booking starts at 10:00 AM IST daily.
  • Non-AC Classes (SL, 2S): Booking starts at 11:00 AM IST daily.

Important Tatkal Rules

  • Booking Window: Opens 1 day in advance of the train's departure from its originating station.
  • No Refunds: No refund is granted on the cancellation of confirmed Tatkal tickets.
  • Limit per PNR: A maximum of 4 passengers can be booked on a single Tatkal PNR.
  • ID Proof: No need to enter ID details during booking, but one passenger must carry a valid original ID during travel.
  • Agent Restrictions: Authorized agents are restricted from booking Tatkal tickets during the first 30 minutes of opening (10:00-10:30 AM and 11:00-11:30 AM).

  • Stable Connection: Use a wired (Ethernet) connection if possible, or ensure you have full 5G/Wi-Fi bars. Every millisecond counts during the initial rush.
