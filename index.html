Indian Railways Booking & Tatkal Guide

What is RailBookingDate?

RailBookingDate is a smart Indian Railway Booking Date Calculator designed to help you plan your train journeys effortlessly. It calculates the exact date when booking opens for your planned travel date based on the 60-day Advance Reservation Period (ARP).

When does Tatkal Booking Open?

Tatkal booking is for travelers who need to book tickets at the last minute. The timings are strict:

  • AC Classes (1A, 2A, 3A, CC, EC, 3E): Booking opens at 10:00 AM, one day before the journey.
  • Non-AC Classes (Sleeper, 2S): Booking opens at 11:00 AM, one day before the journey.

How does the 60-Day Advance Reservation Work?

General booking for Indian trains opens 60 days in advance. The booking window opens sharply at 8:00 AM. For example, if you want to travel on a specific date, you should check our calendar to find the exact date to book your ticket 60 days prior.

Why use a Train Ticket Booking Calculator?

Calculating the 60-day window manually can be confusing. RailBookingDate removes the guesswork by providing an accurate IRCTC booking date calendar, ensuring you never miss the 8:00 AM opening window for your preferred train.
